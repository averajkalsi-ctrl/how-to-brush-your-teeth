How to Brush Your Teeth Properly

A professional blue-themed guide for healthy teeth and fresh breath.

Learn the Steps

Why Brushing Matters

Prevents Cavities

Removes plaque and bacteria that cause tooth decay.

Fresh Breath

Keeps your mouth clean and smelling fresh.

Healthy Gums

Prevents gum disease and inflammation.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Apply a pea-sized amount of toothpaste.
  2. Brush front teeth using gentle circular motions.
  3. Brush back teeth carefully.
  4. Clean chewing surfaces.
  5. Brush your tongue gently.
  6. Rinse thoroughly and clean your brush.
